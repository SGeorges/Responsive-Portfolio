About Me

Hello World! I'm Stefano,


I'm an aspiring web designer looking to make a start from humble (non-existant) beginnings. After going through my developmental years (18-23 for me, late bloomer) under the misconception more difficult meant better for me, I came out with a BS in Chemical Engineering at Drexel University. The road was challenging but once I recieved my degree everything worked out well....


... is not what happened. In a field where I felt my creativity and freedom where restricted I decided to go for broke and change my career pathway to something I personally enjoy. Even though I can't stress how much I loved working outside in the extreme heat or cold, fighting off ticks, chiggers, bees, spiders, and not being able to do anything to please my client because the fact they hired me means they're already pouring money in an undesired cost, I wanted to do something I could enjoy.


So here I am. Thanks for checking out my profile!
